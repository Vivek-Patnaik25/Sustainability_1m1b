# SDG 12: Responsible Consumption and Production

**Goal:** Ensure sustainable consumption and production patterns.

**Relevant Targets for Local Issues:**
- **12.3:** Halve per capita global food waste at the retail and consumer levels.
- **12.5:** Substantially reduce waste generation through prevention, reduction, recycling and reuse.
- **12.8:** Ensure that people everywhere have the relevant information and awareness for sustainable development and lifestyles.

**Key Concepts:**
- Excessive plastic use.
- Food waste.
- Lack of recycling facilities.
- Single-use items (linear economy).
