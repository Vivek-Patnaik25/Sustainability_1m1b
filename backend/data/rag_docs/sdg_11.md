# SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities

**Goal:** Make cities and human settlements inclusive, safe, resilient, and sustainable.

**Relevant Targets for Local Issues:**
- **11.6:** Reduce the adverse per capita environmental impact of cities, focusing on air quality and municipal and other waste management.
- **11.2:** Provide access to safe, affordable, accessible and sustainable transport systems for all, improving road safety.
- **11.5:** Reduce the number of deaths and the number of people affected by disasters, including water-related disasters.
- **11.7:** Provide universal access to safe, inclusive and accessible, green and public spaces.

**Key Concepts:**
- Urban waste management (overflowing bins, litter).
- Air pollution from traffic or burning.
- Lack of green spaces or parks.
- Safety in public spaces (lighting, walkability).
