# SDG 6: Clean Water and Sanitation

**Goal:** Ensure availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all.

**Relevant Targets for Local Issues:**
- **6.1:** Achieve universal and equitable access to safe and affordable drinking water for all.
- **6.3:** Improve water quality by reducing pollution, eliminating dumping and minimizing release of hazardous chemicals and materials.
- **6.4:** Substantially increase water-use efficiency across all sectors and ensure sustainable withdrawals and supply of freshwater.
- **6.6:** Protect and restore water-related ecosystems, including mountains, forests, wetlands, rivers, aquifers and lakes.

**Key Concepts:**
- Water leaks (wastage).
- Contaminated drinking water.
- Sewage overflow or lack of drainage.
- Pollution of local water bodies (lakes, rivers).
